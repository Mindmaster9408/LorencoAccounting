## ğŸ‘¥ Regular User Features

### Company Selection
1. After login, you'll see your assigned company
2. Click **Select Company** to enter the company's system

### Company Dashboard
Once in a company, you can:
- **Manage Employees**: Add, edit, and view employee records
- **Process Pay Runs**: Create and process payroll
- **Configure Payroll Items**: Set up deductions and allowances
- **View Details**: Access individual employee information

### Navigation
- Use the **left sidebar** to navigate between modules
- **Switch Company**: See and select from the next 3 active companies
- **Return to Dashboard**: Go back to company selection
- **Logout**: Exit the system securely
